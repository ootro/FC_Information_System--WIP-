Who we are?

Shooters Sports Club is committed to protecting your privacy. We are a sports organization dedicated to providing high-quality athletic products and services to our customers. We collect personal information to fulfill your orders, to provide customer support, and to improve our products and services. We do not share your information with third parties unless required by law or necessary to provide our services. By using our website, you consent to the terms of this privacy policy.

What we do?

We may collect personal information to provide and improve our products and services, such as processing orders, answering customer inquiries, and communicating with customers about our products and promotions. We may also use personal information to improve our website and tailor our advertising to your interests.

Data you provide us whenever you create an account

We collect and store the information you provide when you create an account with us, such as your name, email address, and password. This information is used to facilitate your access to our services and to communicate with you about your account. We may also use this information to improve our services and to personalize your experience with us. We do not share your account information with third parties unless required by law or as necessary to provide our services to you.

Information we automatically collect

We may automatically collect certain information about your visit to our website, such as your IP address, browser type, device type, operating system type, and other similar analytics. This information is used to improve our website and your browsing experience. We may also use cookies and other tracking technologies to collect information about your online activity and interactions with our website.

Whenever you leave a comment/reply

When you leave a comment/reply on our website, we collect the data shown in the comment/reply form, and also your IP address and browser user agent string to help spam detection.

Cookies that we use

When you visit our website, we may use cookies to collect information about your browsing behavior. This helps us provide you with a more personalized experience and allows us to improve our website. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may limit some functionality on our site.

Third party data collection

We may also collect information from third-party sources, such as social media platforms or other publicly available sources. This information may include your name, email address, demographic information, and other information that you have made publicly available. We may use this information to better understand our users and to provide more personalized content and services. We may also combine this information with other information that we have collected about you to improve our understanding of your interests and preferences.

How safe is your information?

We take the security of your personal information seriously and use industry-standard security measures to protect it from unauthorized access, modification, or disclosure. However, it's important to keep in mind that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security of your information. We advise you to take appropriate measures to protect your personal information, such as using strong passwords and not sharing your login credentials with others.
