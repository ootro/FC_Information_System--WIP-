Full Description

The FCDK 23/24 away game t-shirt is made of light, technological fabric, it is decorated with an embroidered New Balance logo and a FC Dynamo Kyiv chevron is sewn on. Thanks to the NB Dry technology, the fabric of the T-shirt is able to absorb and remove sweat from the skin for quick evaporation, ensuring comfort and dryness. Material: 100% polyester. The color is blue. The basic t-shirt is offered without number and name. All this is offered as an additional option, which is carried out within 1-3 working days.

Featuring a sleek and minimalist design, the FCDK 23/24 away game T-shirt is perfect for player who want to look and feel their best. The t-shirt's fitted shape and racerback design offer maximum support, while its flatlock seams prevent irritation.
